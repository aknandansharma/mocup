About the webinar

The webinar will delve into the intricacies of the BFSI sector's cybersecurity landscape in Saudi Arabia, exploring both the current challenges and the future threats that lie ahead.



The Key Points of Discussion -

  • Analysis of recent cyber threats and attacks targeting banks and financial institutions in Saudi Arabia.
  • Overview of the existing regulatory framework and cybersecurity guidelines for BFSI companies in Saudi Arabia.
  • Discussion on the potential cybersecurity risks associated with the adoption of emerging technologies.
  • Real-world examples of effective incident response and recovery in the aftermath of cyber incidents.
  • Strategies for developing a powerful cybersecurity workforce through training, education, and certifications.


Who is it for?

The webinar on "Cybersecurity Challenges for BFSI Sector in Saudi Arabia" is specifically designed and tailored for IT experts, CISOs (Chief Information Security Officers), CIOs (Chief Information Officers), and Security Experts who are involved in or interested in cybersecurity within the Banking, Financial Services, and Insurance (BFSI) sector in Saudi Arabia
